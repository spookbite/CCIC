                                       Nifty June futures trade at premium
                                    
                                    Jun 12, 2023 04:26 PM | Source: capitalmarket.com
                                        
                                    
                                        
                                         Infosys, HDFC Bank and HAL were the top traded contracts.
                                    
                                   
                                
                                 
                               
                                
                                
                                The Nifty June 2023 futures closed at 18,690.40, a premium of 88.9 points compared with the Nifty's closing of 18,601.50 in the cash market.

In the cash market, the Nifty 50 added 38.10 points or 0.21% to settle at 18,601.50.

The NSE's India VIX, a gauge of market's expectation of volatility over the near term, rose 1.12% to 11.25.
<style='margin-top:96px;'></style>
Infosys, HDFC Bank and Hindustan Aeronautics (HAL) were the top traded individual stock futures contracts in F&O segment of NSE.

The June 2023 F&O contracts will expire on 29 June 2023.
