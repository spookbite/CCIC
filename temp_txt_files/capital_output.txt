                                       Nifty May futures trade at premium
                                    
                                    May 23, 2023 04:30 PM | Source: capitalmarket.com
                                        
                                    
                                        
                                         Adani Ent, Adani Ports and SEZ and RIL were the top traded contracts.
                                    
                                   
                                
                                 
                               
                                
                                
                                The Nifty May 2023 futures closed at 18,359, a premium of 11 points compared with the Nifty's closing of 18,348 in the cash market.

In the cash market, the Nifty 50 advanced 33.60 points or 0.18% to settle at 18,348.

The NSE's India VIX, a gauge of market's expectation of volatility over the near term, advanced 0.27% to 12.61.
<style='margin-top:96px;'></style>
Adani Enterprises, Adani Ports and Special Economic Zone and Reliance Industries were the top traded individual stock futures contracts in F&O segment of NSE.

The May 2023 F&O contracts will expire on 25 May 2023.
