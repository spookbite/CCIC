                                       Nifty July futures trade at premium
                                    
                                    Jul 24, 2023 04:26 PM | Source: capitalmarket.com
                                        
                                    
                                        
                                         NSE India VIX rose 1.45% to 11.65.
                                    
                                   
                                
                                 
                               
                                
                                
                                
The Nifty July 2023 futures closed at 19,681.60, a premium of 9.25 points compared with the Nifty's closing 19,672.35 in the cash market.
  
In the cash market, the Nifty 50 declined 72.65 points or 0.37% to settle at 19,672.35.
<style='margin-top:96px;'></style>
The NSE's India VIX, a gauge of market's expectation of volatility over the near term, rose 1.45% to 11.65.

ITC, HDFC Bank, and ICICI Bank were the top traded individual stock futures contracts in F&O segment of NSE.

The July 2023 F&O contracts will expire on 27 July 2023.
