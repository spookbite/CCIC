                                       Nifty July futures trade at premium
                                    
                                    Jun 30, 2023 04:17 PM | Source: capitalmarket.com
                                        
                                    
                                        
                                         Infosys, HDFC Bank, and Tata Consultancy Services (TCS) were the top traded contracts.
                                    
                                   
                                
                                 
                               
                                
                                
                                
The Nifty July 2023 futures closed at 19,270.80, a premium of 81.75 points compared with the Nifty's closing of 19,189.05 in the cash market.
  
In the cash market, the Nifty 50 advanced 216.95 points or 1.14% to settle at 19,189.05.
<style='margin-top:96px;'></style>
The NSE's India VIX, a gauge of market's expectation of volatility over the near term, declined 0.80% to 10.80.

Infosys, HDFC Bank and TCS were the top traded individual stock futures contracts in F&O segment of NSE.

The July 2023 F&O contracts will expire on 27 July 2023.
