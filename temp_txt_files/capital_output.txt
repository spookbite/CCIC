                                       Nifty May futures trade at premium
                                    
                                    Apr 28, 2023 04:20 PM | Source: capitalmarket.com
                                        
                                    
                                        
                                         NSE India VIX tumbled 4.17% as shares rallied.
                                    
                                   
                                
                                 
                               
                                
                                
                                
The Nifty May 2023 futures closed at 18,113.45, a premium of 48.45 points compared with the Nifty's closing of 18,065 in the cash market.

In the cash market, the Nifty 50 advanced 149.95 points or 0.84% to settle at 18,065.
<style='margin-top:96px;'></style>
The NSE's India VIX, a gauge of market's expectation of volatility over the near term, dropped 4.17% to 10.95.

Axis Bank, Reliance Industries (RIL), and Adani Enterprises were the top traded individual stock futures contracts in F&O segment of NSE.

The May 2023 F&O contracts will expire on 25 May 2023.
