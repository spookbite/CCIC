                                       Nifty May futures trade at premium
                                    
                                    May 18, 2023 04:28 PM | Source: capitalmarket.com
                                        
                                    
                                        
                                         NSE India VIX dropped 2.36% to 12.80.
                                    
                                   
                                
                                 
                               
                                
                                
                                
The Nifty May 2023 futures closed at 18,178.90, a premium of 48.95 points compared with the Nifty's closing of 18,129.95 in the cash market.

In the cash market, the Nifty 50 declined 51.80 points or 0.28% to settle at 18,129.95.
<style='margin-top:96px;'></style>
The NSE's India VIX, a gauge of market's expectation of volatility over the near term, slipped 2.36% to 12.80.

State Bank of India (SBI), ITC and HDFC Bank were the top traded individual stock futures contracts in F&O segment of NSE.

The May 2023 F&O contracts will expire on 25 May 2023.
