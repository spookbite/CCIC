                                       Nifty May futures trade at premium
                                    
                                    May 02, 2023 04:32 PM | Source: capitalmarket.com
                                        
                                    
                                        
                                         NSE India VIX soared 8.67% to 11.90.
                                    
                                   
                                
                                 
                               
                                
                                
                                The Nifty May 2023 futures closed at 18,203.95, a premium of 56.3 points compared with the Nifty's closing of 18,147.65 in the cash market.

In the cash market, the Nifty 50 advanced 82.65 points or 0.46% to settle at 18,147.65.

The NSE's India VIX, a gauge of market's expectation of volatility over the near term, zoomed 8.67% to 11.90.
<style='margin-top:96px;'></style>
Reliance Industries (RIL), Kotak Mahindra Bank and Infosys were the top traded individual stock futures contracts in F&O segment of NSE.

The May 2023 F&O contracts will expire on 25 May 2023.
