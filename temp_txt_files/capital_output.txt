                                       Nifty September futures trade at premium
                                    
                                    Sep 15, 2023 05:14 PM | Source: capitalmarket.com
                                        
                                    
                                        
                                         HDFC Bank, RIL & ICICI Bank were the top traded contracts.
                                    
                                   
                                
                                 
                               
                                
                                
                                
The Nifty September 2023 futures closed at 20,240.10, a premium of 47.75 points compared with the Nifty's closing 20,192.35 in the cash market.

In the cash market, the Nifty 50 gained 89.25 points or 0.44% to settle at 20,192.35.
<style='margin-top:96px;'></style>
The NSE's India VIX, a gauge of market's expectation of volatility over the near term, fell 3.66% to 10.90.

HDFC Bank, Reliance Industries (RIL) and ICICI Bank were the top traded individual stock futures contracts in F&O segment of NSE.

The September 2023 F&O contracts will expire on 28 September 2023.
