                                       Nifty June futures trade at premium 
                                    
                                    Jun 21, 2023 04:32 PM | Source: capitalmarket.com
                                        
                                    
                                        
                                         HDFC Bank, Piramal Enterprises, Shriram Finance were the top traded contracts. 
                                    
                                   
                                
                                 
                               
                                
                                
                                The Nifty June 2023 futures closed at 18,898.95, a premium of 42.1 points compared with the Nifty's closing of 18,856.85 in the cash market.

In the cash market, the Nifty 50 advanced 40.15 points or 0.21% to settle at 18,856.85.

The NSE's India VIX, a gauge of market's expectation of volatility over the near term, advanced 1.43% to 11.29.
<style='margin-top:96px;'></style>
HDFC Bank, Piramal Enterprises, Shriram Finance were the top traded individual stock futures contracts in F&O segment of NSE.

The June 2023 F&O contracts will expire on 29 June 2023.
