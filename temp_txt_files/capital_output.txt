                                       Nifty March futures trade at premium
                                    
                                    Mar 16, 2023 04:33 PM | Source: capitalmarket.com
                                        
                                    
                                        
                                         Reliance Industries (RIL), HDFC Bank and ICICI Bank were the top traded contracts.
                                    
                                   
                                
                                 
                               
                                
                                
                                
The Nifty March 2023 futures closed at 17,070.35, a premium of 84.75 points compared with the Nifty's closing of 16,985.60 in the cash market.

Turnover on the National Stock Exchange's futures & options (F&O) segment was Rs 474.32 lakh crore compared with Rs 218.54 lakh crore reported in the previous session.
<style='margin-top:96px;'></style>
In the cash market, the Nifty 50 rose 13.45 points or 0.08% to settle at 16,985.60.

The NSE's India VIX, a gauge of market's expectation of volatility over the near term, declined 0.47% to 16.22.

RIL, HDFC Bank and ICICI Bank were the top traded individual stock futures contracts in F&O segment of NSE.

The March 2023 F&O contracts will expire on 29 March 2023.
