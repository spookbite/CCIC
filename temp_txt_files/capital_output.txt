                                       Nifty June futures trade at steep premium
                                    
                                    May 30, 2023 04:27 PM | Source: capitalmarket.com
                                        
                                    
                                        
                                         HDFC Bank, Apollo Hospitals & Kotak Bank were the top traded contracts. 
                                    
                                   
                                
                                 
                               
                                
                                
                                
The Nifty June 2023 futures closed at 18,719.65, a premium of 85.8 points compared with the Nifty's closing of 18,633.85 in the cash market.

In the cash market, the Nifty 50 gained 35.20 points or 0.19% to settle at 18,633.85.
<style='margin-top:96px;'></style>
The NSE's India VIX, a gauge of market's expectation of volatility over the near term, fell 2.33% to 12.01.

HDFC Bank, Apollo Hospitals Enterprise and Kotak Mahindra Bank were the top traded individual stock futures contracts in F&O segment of NSE.

The June 2023 F&O contracts will expire on 29 June 2023.
