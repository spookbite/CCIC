                                       Nifty May futures trade at premium
                                    
                                    May 22, 2023 04:15 PM | Source: capitalmarket.com
                                        
                                    
                                        
                                         NSE India VIX rose 2.17% to 12.57.
                                    
                                   
                                
                                 
                               
                                
                                
                                The Nifty May 2023 futures closed at 18,324.55, a premium of 10.15 points compared with the Nifty's closing of 18,314.40 in the cash market.

In the cash market, the Nifty 50 advanced 111 points or 0.61% to settle at 18,314.40.

The NSE's India VIX, a gauge of market's expectation of volatility over the near term, advanced 2.17% to 12.57.
<style='margin-top:96px;'></style>
Adani Enterprises, Adani Ports and Special Economic Zone and HDFC Bank were the top traded individual stock futures contracts in F&O segment of NSE.

The May 2023 F&O contracts will expire on 25 May 2023.
