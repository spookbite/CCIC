                                       Nifty June futures trade at premium
                                    
                                    Jun 09, 2023 04:38 PM | Source: capitalmarket.com
                                        
                                    
                                        
                                         NSE India VIX declined 1.22% to 11.12.
                                    
                                   
                                
                                 
                               
                                
                                
                                
The Nifty June 2023 futures closed at 18,627.55, a premium of 64.15 points compared with the Nifty's closing of 18,563.40 in the cash market.

In the cash market, the Nifty 50 lost 71.15 points or 0.38% to settle at 18,563.40.
<style='margin-top:96px;'></style>
The NSE's India VIX, a gauge of market's expectation of volatility over the near term, fell 1.22% to 11.12.

Kotak Mahindra Bank, Hindustan Aeronautics (HAL) and Indian Energy Exchange (IEX) were the top traded individual stock futures contracts in F&O segment of NSE.

The June 2023 F&O contracts will expire on 29 June 2023.
