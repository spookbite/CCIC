                                       Nifty June futures trade at premium
                                    
                                    May 25, 2023 04:20 PM | Source: capitalmarket.com
                                        
                                    
                                        
                                         NSE India VIX slumped 4.51% as shares advanced.
                                    
                                   
                                
                                 
                               
                                
                                
                                The Nifty June 2023 futures closed at 18,428, a premium of 106.85 points compared with the Nifty's closing of 18,321.15 in the cash market.

In the cash market, the Nifty 50 rose 35.75 points or 0.20% to settle at 18,321.15.

The NSE's India VIX, a gauge of market's expectation of volatility over the near term, slipped 4.51% to 12.52.
<style='margin-top:96px;'></style>
Reliance Industries, HDFC Bank and ICICI Bank were the top traded individual stock futures contracts in F&O segment of NSE.

The June 2023 F&O contracts will expire on 29 June 2023.
