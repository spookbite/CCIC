                                       Nifty January futures trade at premium
                                    
                                    Jan 18, 2023 04:40 PM | Source: capitalmarket.com
                                        
                                    
                                        
                                         NSE India VIX fell 1.49% as shares advanced.
                                    
                                   
                                
                                 
                               
                                
                                
                                The Nifty January 2023 futures closed at 18,204.10, a premium of 38.75 points compared with the Nifty's closing of 18,165.35 in the cash market.

Turnover on the National Stock Exchange's futures & options (F&O) segment was Rs 169.07 lakh crore compared with Rs 219.64 lakh crore reported in the previous session.

In the cash market, the Nifty 50 added 112.05 points or 0.62% to settle at 18,165.35.
<style='margin-top:96px;'></style>
The NSE's India VIX, a gauge of market's expectation of volatility over the near term, declined 1.49% to 14.3725.

Adani Enterprises, HDFC Bank and Reliance Industries were the top traded individual stock futures contracts in F&O segment of NSE.

The January 2023 F&O contracts will expire on 25 January 2023.
