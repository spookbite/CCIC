                                       Nifty June futures trade at premium
                                    
                                    Jun 19, 2023 04:17 PM | Source: capitalmarket.com
                                        
                                    
                                        
                                         NSE India VIX climbed 3.55% as shares declined.
                                    
                                   
                                
                                 
                               
                                
                                
                                
The Nifty June 2023 futures closed at 18,840.40, a premium of 84.95 points compared with the Nifty's closing of 18,755.45 in the cash market.
  
In the cash market, the Nifty 50 lost 70.55 points or 0.37% to settle at 18,755.45.
<style='margin-top:96px;'></style>
The NSE's India VIX, a gauge of market's expectation of volatility over the near term, rallied 3.55% to 11.23.

Adani Enterprises, HDFC Bank and ICICI Bank were the top traded individual stock futures contracts in F&O segment of NSE.

The June 2023 F&O contracts will expire on 29 June 2023.
