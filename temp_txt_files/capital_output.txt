                                       Nifty August futures trade at premium
                                    
                                    Aug 01, 2023 05:13 PM | Source: capitalmarket.com
                                        
                                    
                                        
                                         Maruti, HeroMoto and RIL were the top traded contracts. 
                                    
                                   
                                
                                 
                               
                                
                                
                                
The Nifty August 2023 futures closed at 19,810.30, a premium of 76.75 points compared with the Nifty's closing 19,733.55 in the cash market.

In the cash market, the Nifty 50 fell 20.25 points or 0.1% to settle at 19,733.55.
<style='margin-top:96px;'></style>
The NSE's India VIX, a gauge of market's expectation of volatility over the near term, fell 1.24% to 10.28.

Maruti Suzuki India, Hero MotoCorp and Reliance Industries were the top traded individual stock futures contracts in F&O segment of NSE.

The August 2023 F&O contracts will expire on 31 August 2023.
