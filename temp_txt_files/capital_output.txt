                                       Nifty August futures trade at premium
                                    
                                    Aug 16, 2023 04:25 PM | Source: capitalmarket.com
                                        
                                    
                                        
                                         RIL, Infosys & HDFC Bank were the top traded contracts.
                                    
                                   
                                
                                 
                               
                                
                                
                                The Nifty August 2023 futures closed at 19,466, a premium of 1 point compared with the Nifty's closing 19,465 in the cash market.

In the cash market, the Nifty 50 added 30.45 points or 0.16% to settle at 19,465.

The NSE's India VIX, a gauge of market's expectation of volatility over the near term, rose 1.08% to 12.13.
<style='margin-top:96px;'></style>
Reliance Industries (RIL), Infosys and HDFC Bank were the top traded individual stock futures contracts in F&O segment of NSE.

The August 2023 F&O contracts will expire on 31 August 2023.
