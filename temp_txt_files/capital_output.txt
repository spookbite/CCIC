                                       Nifty June futures trade at premium 
                                    
                                    Jun 23, 2023 04:45 PM | Source: capitalmarket.com
                                        
                                    
                                        
                                         Adani Enterprises, HDFC Bank, ICICI Bank were the top traded contracts. 
                                    
                                   
                                
                                 
                               
                                
                                
                                The Nifty June 2023 futures closed at 18,700.50, a premium of 35 points compared with the Nifty's closing of 18,665.50 in the cash market.

In the cash market, the Nifty 50 declined 105.75 points or 0.56% to settle at 18,665.50.

The NSE's India VIX, a gauge of market's expectation of volatility over the near term, declined 2.70% to 11.24.
<style='margin-top:96px;'></style>
Adani Enterprises, HDFC Bank, ICICI Bank were the top traded individual stock futures contracts in F&O segment of NSE.

The June 2023 F&O contracts will expire on 29 June 2023.
