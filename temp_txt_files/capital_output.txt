                                       Nifty June futures trade at premium 
                                    
                                    Jun 22, 2023 04:37 PM | Source: capitalmarket.com
                                        
                                    
                                        
                                         NSE India VIX climbed 2.25% as shares declined.
                                    
                                   
                                
                                 
                               
                                
                                
                                The Nifty June 2023 futures closed at 18,835.15, a premium of 63.9 points compared with the Nifty's closing of 18,771.25 in the cash market.

In the cash market, the Nifty 50 declined 85.60 points or 0.45% to settle at 18,771.25.

The NSE's India VIX, a gauge of market's expectation of volatility over the near term, advanced 2.25% to 11.55.
<style='margin-top:96px;'></style>
HDFC Bank, ICICI Bank, Infosys were the top traded individual stock futures contracts in F&O segment of NSE.

The June 2023 F&O contracts will expire on 29 June 2023.
