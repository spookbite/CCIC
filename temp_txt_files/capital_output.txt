                                       Nifty February futures trade at premium
                                    
                                    Feb 13, 2023 04:49 PM | Source: capitalmarket.com
                                        
                                    
                                        
                                         AdaniEnt, Adani Ports and RIL were the top traded contracts. 
                                    
                                   
                                
                                 
                               
                                
                                
                                
The Nifty February 2023 futures closed at 17,799.05, a premium of 28.15 points compared with the Nifty's closing of 17,770.90 in the cash market.

Turnover on the National Stock Exchange's futures & options (F&O) segment was Rs 119.44 lakh crore compared with Rs 80.57 lakh crore reported in the previous session.
<style='margin-top:96px;'></style>
In the cash market, the Nifty 50 lost 85.60 points or 0.48% to settle at 17,770.90.

The NSE's India VIX, a gauge of market's expectation of volatility over the near term, jumped 7.33% to 13.68.

Adani Enterprises, Adani Ports and Special Economic Zone and Reliance Industries were the top traded individual stock futures contracts in F&O segment of NSE.

The February 2023 F&O contracts will expire on 23 February 2023.
