                                       Nifty September futures trade at premium
                                    
                                    Sep 06, 2023 04:31 PM | Source: capitalmarket.com
                                        
                                    
                                        
                                         NSE India VIX fell 1.27% as shares advanced.
                                    
                                   
                                
                                 
                               
                                
                                
                                The Nifty September 2023 futures closed at 19,666.20, a premium of 55.15 points compared with the Nifty's closing 19,611.05 in the cash market.

In the cash market, the Nifty 50 rose 36.15 points or 0.18% to settle at 19,611.05.

The NSE's India VIX, a gauge of market's expectation of volatility over the near term, declined 1.27% to 10.68.
<style='margin-top:96px;'></style>
HDFC Bank, ICICI Bank and Axis Bank were the top traded individual stock futures contracts in F&O segment of NSE.

The September 2023 F&O contracts will expire on 28 September 2023.
