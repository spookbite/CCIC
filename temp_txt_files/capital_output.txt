                                       Nifty June futures trade at premium
                                    
                                    Jun 05, 2023 04:12 PM | Source: capitalmarket.com
                                        
                                    
                                        
                                         Reliance Industries (RIL), Tata Chemicals and ICICI Bank were the top traded contracts.
                                    
                                   
                                
                                 
                               
                                
                                
                                
The Nifty June 2023 futures closed at 18,702, a premium of 108.15 points compared with the Nifty's closing of 18,593.85 in the cash market.

In the cash market, the Nifty 50 gained 59.75 points or 0.32% to settle at 18,593.85.
<style='margin-top:96px;'></style>
The NSE's India VIX, a gauge of market's expectation of volatility over the near term, added 0.11% to 11.14.

RIL, Tata Chemicals and ICICI Bank were the top traded individual stock futures contracts in F&O segment of NSE.

The June 2023 F&O contracts will expire on 29 June 2023.
