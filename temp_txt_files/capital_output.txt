                                       Nifty July futures trade at discount
                                    
                                    Jul 26, 2023 04:23 PM | Source: capitalmarket.com
                                        
                                    
                                        
                                         NSE India VIX advanced 2.09% to 10.46.
                                    
                                   
                                
                                 
                               
                                
                                
                                The Nifty July 2023 futures closed at 19,772, a discount of 6.3 points compared with the Nifty's closing 19,778.30 in the cash market.

In the cash market, the Nifty 50 added 97.70 points or 0.5% to settle at 19,778.30.

The NSE's India VIX, a gauge of market's expectation of volatility over the near term, rose 2.09% to 10.46.
<style='margin-top:96px;'></style>
HDFC Bank, Bajaj Finance and Tata Motors were the top traded individual stock futures contracts in F&O segment of NSE.

The July 2023 F&O contracts will expire on 27 July 2023.
