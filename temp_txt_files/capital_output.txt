                                       Nifty March futures trade at premium
                                    
                                    Feb 27, 2023 04:20 PM | Source: capitalmarket.com
                                        
                                    
                                        
                                         NSE India VIX fell 2.18% to 13.88.
                                    
                                   
                                
                                 
                               
                                
                                
                                The Nifty March 2023 futures closed at 17,509, a premium of 116.3 points compared with the Nifty's closing of 17,392.70 in the cash market.

Turnover on the National Stock Exchange's futures & options (F&O) segment was Rs 154.12 lakh crore compared with Rs 114.70 lakh crore reported in the previous session.

In the cash market, the Nifty 50 shed 73.10 points or 0.42% to settle at 17,392.70.
<style='margin-top:96px;'></style>
The NSE's India VIX, a gauge of market's expectation of volatility over the near term, fell 2.18% to 13.8750.

Adani Enterprises, Reliance Industries (RIL) and HDFC Bank were the top traded individual stock futures contracts in F&O segment of NSE.

The March 2023 F&O contracts will expire on 29 March 2023.
