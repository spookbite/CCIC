                                       Nifty May futures trade at premium
                                    
                                    May 15, 2023 04:17 PM | Source: capitalmarket.com
                                        
                                    
                                        
                                         NSE India VIX jumped 2.48% to 13.17.
                                    
                                   
                                
                                 
                               
                                
                                
                                
The Nifty May 2023 futures closed at 18,408, a premium of 9.15 points compared with the Nifty's closing of 18,398.85 in the cash market.

In the cash market, the Nifty 50 advanced 84.05 points or 0.46% to settle at 18,398.85.
<style='margin-top:96px;'></style>
The NSE's India VIX, a gauge of market's expectation of volatility over the near term, rose 2.48% to 13.17.

Tata Motors, DLF and Adani Enterprises were the top traded individual stock futures contracts in F&O segment of NSE.

The May 2023 F&O contracts will expire on 25 May 2023.
