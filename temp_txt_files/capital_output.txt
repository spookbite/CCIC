                                       Nifty August futures trade at premium
                                    
                                    Aug 29, 2023 04:34 PM | Source: capitalmarket.com
                                        
                                    
                                        
                                         NSE India VIX declined 1.39% to 12.23.
                                    
                                   
                                
                                 
                               
                                
                                
                                
The Nifty August 2023 futures closed at 19,345.75, a premium of 3.1 points compared with the Nifty's closing 19,342.65 in the cash market.

In the cash market, the Nifty 50 added 36.60 points or 0.19% to settle at 19,342.65.
<style='margin-top:96px;'></style>
The NSE's India VIX, a gauge of market's expectation of volatility over the near term, fell 1.39% to 12.23.

Reliance Industries, HDFC Bank and ICICI Bank were the top traded individual stock futures contracts in F&O segment of NSE.

The August 2023 F&O contracts will expire on 31 August 2023.
