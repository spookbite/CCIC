                                       Nifty April futures trade at premium
                                    
                                    Apr 17, 2023 04:04 PM | Source: capitalmarket.com
                                        
                                    
                                        
                                         Infosys, HDFC Bank and TCS were the top traded contracts. 
                                    
                                   
                                
                                 
                               
                                
                                
                                
The Nifty April 2023 futures closed at 17,751, a premium of 44.15 points compared with the Nifty's closing of 17,706.85 in the cash market.

In the cash market, the Nifty 50 declined 121.15 points or 0.68% to settle at 17,706.85.
<style='margin-top:96px;'></style>
The NSE's India VIX, a gauge of market's expectation of volatility over the near term, rose 3.21% to 12.29.

Infosys, HDFC Bank and Tata Consultancy Services (TCS) were the top traded individual stock futures contracts in F&O segment of NSE.

The April 2023 F&O contracts will expire on 27 April 2023.
