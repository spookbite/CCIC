                                       Nifty September futures trade at premium
                                    
                                    Sep 08, 2023 04:56 PM | Source: capitalmarket.com
                                        
                                    
                                        
                                         RIL, HDFC Bank & IRCTC were the top traded contracts.
                                    
                                   
                                
                                 
                               
                                
                                
                                The Nifty September 2023 futures closed at 19,874, a premium of 54.05 points compared with the Nifty's closing 19,819.95 in the cash market.

In the cash market, the Nifty 50 rose 92.90 points or 0.47% to settle at 19,819.95.

The NSE's India VIX, a gauge of market's expectation of volatility over the near term, declined 0.85% to 10.78.
<style='margin-top:96px;'></style>
Reliance Industries (RIL), HDFC Bank and Indian Railway Catering and Tourism Corporation (IRCTC) were the top traded individual stock futures contracts in F&O segment of NSE.

The September 2023 F&O contracts will expire on 28 September 2023.
