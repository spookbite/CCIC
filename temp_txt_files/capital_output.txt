                                       Nifty August futures trade at premium
                                    
                                    Aug 02, 2023 04:18 PM | Source: capitalmarket.com
                                        
                                    
                                        
                                         NSE India VIX soared 9.67% to 11.28.
                                    
                                   
                                
                                 
                               
                                
                                
                                
The Nifty August 2023 futures closed at 19,585.15, a premium of 58.6 points compared with the Nifty's closing 19,526.55 in the cash market.

In the cash market, the Nifty 50 slipped 207 points or 1.05% to settle at 19,526.55.
<style='margin-top:96px;'></style>
The NSE's India VIX, a gauge of market's expectation of volatility over the near term, surged 9.67% to 11.28.

Reliance Industries, HDFC Bank and ICICI Bank were the top traded individual stock futures contracts in F&O segment of NSE.

The August 2023 F&O contracts will expire on 31 August 2023.
