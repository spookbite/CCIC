                                       Nifty June futures trade at premium
                                    
                                    Jun 08, 2023 04:22 PM | Source: capitalmarket.com
                                        
                                    
                                        
                                         Kotak Mahindra Bank, HDFC Bank and Reliance Industries (RIL) were the top traded contracts.
                                    
                                   
                                
                                 
                               
                                
                                
                                
The Nifty June 2023 futures closed at 18,722, a premium of 87.45 points compared with the Nifty's closing of 18,634.55 in the cash market.

In the cash market, the Nifty 50 lost 91.85 points or 0.49% to settle at 18,634.55.
<style='margin-top:96px;'></style>
The NSE's India VIX, a gauge of market's expectation of volatility over the near term, declined 1.61% to 11.26.

Kotak Mahindra Bank, HDFC Bank and RIL were the top traded individual stock futures contracts in F&O segment of NSE.

The June 2023 F&O contracts will expire on 29 June 2023.
