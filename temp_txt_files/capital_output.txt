                                       Nifty April futures trade at premium
                                    
                                    Apr 11, 2023 04:24 PM | Source: capitalmarket.com
                                        
                                    
                                        
                                         NSE India VIX fell 2.42% as shares advanced.
                                    
                                   
                                
                                 
                               
                                
                                
                                The Nifty April 2023 futures closed at 17,787, a premium of 64.7 points compared with the Nifty's closing of 17,722.30 in the cash market.

In the cash market, the Nifty 50 gained 98.25 points or 0.56% to settle at 17,722.30.

The NSE's India VIX, a gauge of market's expectation of volatility over the near term, declined 2.42% to 11.98.
<style='margin-top:96px;'></style>
Kotak Mahindra Bank, ICICI Bank and Infosys were the top traded individual stock futures contracts in F&O segment of NSE.

The April 2023 F&O contracts will expire on 27 April 2023.
