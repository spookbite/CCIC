                                       Nifty May futures trade at premium
                                    
                                    May 16, 2023 04:24 PM | Source: capitalmarket.com
                                        
                                    
                                        
                                         Reliance Industries (RIL), HDFC Bank and ICICI Bank were the top traded contracts.
                                    
                                   
                                
                                 
                               
                                
                                
                                
The Nifty May 2023 futures closed at 18,325.10, a premium of 38.6 points compared with the Nifty's closing of 18,286.50 in the cash market.

In the cash market, the Nifty 50 slipped 112.35 points or 0.61% to settle at 18,286.50.
<style='margin-top:96px;'></style>
The NSE's India VIX, a gauge of market's expectation of volatility over the near term, advanced 0.91% to 13.29.

RIL, HDFC Bank and ICICI Bank were the top traded individual stock futures contracts in F&O segment of NSE.

The May 2023 F&O contracts will expire on 25 May 2023.
