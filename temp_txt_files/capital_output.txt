                                       Nifty July futures trade at premium
                                    
                                    Jun 28, 2023 04:18 PM | Source: capitalmarket.com
                                        
                                    
                                        
                                         Reliance Industries (RIL), HDFC Bank and Infosys were the top traded contracts.
                                    
                                   
                                
                                 
                               
                                
                                
                                
The Nifty July 2023 futures closed at 19,082.35, a premium of 110.25 points compared with the Nifty's closing of 18,972.10 in the cash market.
  
In the cash market, the Nifty 50 advanced 154.70 points or 0.82% to settle at 18,972.10.
<style='margin-top:96px;'></style>
The NSE's India VIX, a gauge of market's expectation of volatility over the near term, rose 1.02% to 10.89.

RIL, HDFC Bank, and Infosys were the top traded individual stock futures contracts in F&O segment of NSE.

The July 2023 F&O contracts will expire on 27 July 2023.
