                                       Nifty May futures trade at premium
                                    
                                    Apr 27, 2023 04:09 PM | Source: capitalmarket.com
                                        
                                    
                                        
                                         NSE India VIX slipped 1.95% as shares advanced.
                                    
                                   
                                
                                 
                               
                                
                                
                                
The Nifty May 2023 futures closed at 17,995.80, a premium of 80.75 points compared with the Nifty's closing of 17,915.05 in the cash market.

In the cash market, the Nifty 50 gained 101.45 points or 0.57% to settle at 17,915.05.
<style='margin-top:96px;'></style>
The NSE's India VIX, a gauge of market's expectation of volatility over the near term, declined 1.95% to 11.43.

Reliance Industries (RIL), Infosys and ICICI Bank were the top traded individual stock futures contracts in F&O segment of NSE.

The May 2023 F&O contracts will expire on 25 May 2023.
