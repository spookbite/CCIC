                                       Nifty August futures trade at premium
                                    
                                    Jul 28, 2023 04:47 PM | Source: capitalmarket.com
                                        
                                    
                                        
                                         NSE India VIX slipped 3.59% to 10.14.
                                    
                                   
                                
                                 
                               
                                
                                
                                The Nifty August 2023 futures closed at 19,733.30, a premium of 87.25 points compared with the Nifty's closing 19,646.05 in the cash market.

In the cash market, the Nifty 50 lost 13.85 points or 0.07% to settle at 19,646.05.

The NSE's India VIX, a gauge of market's expectation of volatility over the near term, declined 3.59% to 10.14.
<style='margin-top:96px;'></style>
HDFC Bank, Reliance Industries and Tata Power Company were the top traded individual stock futures contracts in F&O segment of NSE.

The August 2023 F&O contracts will expire on 31 August 2023.
