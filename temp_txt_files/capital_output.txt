                                       Nifty August futures trade at premium
                                    
                                    Aug 17, 2023 04:36 PM | Source: capitalmarket.com
                                        
                                    
                                        
                                         RIL, HDFC Bank & ICICI Bank were the top traded contracts.
                                    
                                   
                                
                                 
                               
                                
                                
                                The Nifty August 2023 futures closed at 19,385, a premium of 19.75 point compared with the Nifty's closing 19,365.25 in the cash market.

In the cash market, the Nifty 50 lost 99.75 points or 0.51% to settle at 19,365.25.

The NSE's India VIX, a gauge of market's expectation of volatility over the near term, rose 0.94% to 12.24.
<style='margin-top:96px;'></style>
Reliance Industries (RIL), HDFC Bank, ICICI Bank were the top traded individual stock futures contracts in F&O segment of NSE.

The August 2023 F&O contracts will expire on 31 August 2023.
