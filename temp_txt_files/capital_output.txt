                                       Nifty July futures trade at discount
                                    
                                    Jul 20, 2023 04:24 PM | Source: capitalmarket.com
                                        
                                    
                                        
                                         HDFC Bank, Infosys and Reliance Industries (RIL) were the top traded contracts.
                                    
                                   
                                
                                 
                               
                                
                                
                                
The Nifty July 2023 futures closed at 19,967.10, a discount of 12.05 points compared with the Nifty's closing of 19,979.15 in the cash market.
  
In the cash market, the Nifty 50 advanced 146 points or 0.74% to settle at 19,979.15.
<style='margin-top:96px;'></style>
The NSE's India VIX, a gauge of market's expectation of volatility over the near term, rose 1.59% to 11.79.

HDFC Bank, Infosys and RIL were the top traded individual stock futures contracts in F&O segment of NSE.

The July 2023 F&O contracts will expire on 27 July 2023.
