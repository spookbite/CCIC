                                       Nifty June futures trade at premium
                                    
                                    Jun 06, 2023 04:20 PM | Source: capitalmarket.com
                                        
                                    
                                        
                                         NSE India VIX rallied 2.17% to 11.38.
                                    
                                   
                                
                                 
                               
                                
                                
                                
The Nifty June 2023 futures closed at 18,683, a premium of 84 points compared with the Nifty's closing of 18,599 in the cash market.

In the cash market, the Nifty 50 added 5.15 points or 0.03% to settle at 18,599.
<style='margin-top:96px;'></style>
The NSE's India VIX, a gauge of market's expectation of volatility over the near term, advanced 2.17% to 11.38.

Infosys, HDFC Bank and Reliance Industries (RIL) were the top traded individual stock futures contracts in F&O segment of NSE.

The June 2023 F&O contracts will expire on 29 June 2023.
