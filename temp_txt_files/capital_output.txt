                                       Nifty May futures trade at premium 
                                    
                                    May 08, 2023 04:30 PM | Source: capitalmarket.com
                                        
                                    
                                        
                                         HDFC Bank, Canara Bank, ICICI Bank were the top traded contracts.
                                    
                                   
                                
                                 
                               
                                
                                
                                The Nifty May 2023 futures closed at 18,317.50, a premium of 53.1 points compared with the Nifty's closing of 18,264.40 in the cash market.

In the cash market, the Nifty 50 added 195.40 points or 1.08% to settle at 18,264.40.

The NSE's India VIX, a gauge of market's expectation of volatility over the near term, advanced 2.76% to 12.64.
<style='margin-top:96px;'></style>
HDFC Bank, Canara Bank, ICICI Bank were the top traded individual stock futures contracts in F&O segment of NSE.

The May 2023 F&O contracts will expire on 25 May 2023.
