                                       Nifty August futures trade at premium
                                    
                                    Jul 27, 2023 04:34 PM | Source: capitalmarket.com
                                        
                                    
                                        
                                         HDFC Bank, Axis Bank & TechM were the traded contracts.
                                    
                                   
                                
                                 
                               
                                
                                
                                The Nifty August 2023 futures closed at 19,840, a premium of 180.1 points compared with the Nifty's closing 19,659.90 in the cash market.

In the cash market, the Nifty 50 lost 118.40 points or 0.6% to settle at 19,659.90.

The NSE's India VIX, a gauge of market's expectation of volatility over the near term, rose 0.52% to 10.51.
<style='margin-top:96px;'></style>
HDFC Bank, Axis Bank and Tech Mahindra were the top traded individual stock futures contracts in F&O segment of NSE.

The August 2023 F&O contracts will expire on 31 August 2023.
