                                       Nifty June futures trade at premium 
                                    
                                    Jun 02, 2023 04:44 PM | Source: capitalmarket.com
                                        
                                    
                                        
                                         NSE India VIX dropped 4.07% as shares advanced.
                                    
                                   
                                
                                 
                               
                                
                                
                                The Nifty June 2023 futures closed at 18,629.95, a premium of 95.85 points compared with the Nifty's closing of 18,534.10 in the cash market.

In the cash market, the Nifty 50 advanced 46.35 points or 0.25% to settle at 18,534.10.

The NSE's India VIX, a gauge of market's expectation of volatility over the near term, dropped 4.07% to 11.13.
<style='margin-top:96px;'></style>
Reliance Industries, ICICI Bank, HDFC Bank, were the top traded individual stock futures contracts in F&O segment of NSE.

The June 2023 F&O contracts will expire on 29 June 2023.
