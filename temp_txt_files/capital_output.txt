                                       Nifty June futures trade at premium
                                    
                                    Jun 15, 2023 04:36 PM | Source: capitalmarket.com
                                        
                                    
                                        
                                         HDFC Bank, RIL and ICICI Bank were the top traded contracts.
                                    
                                   
                                
                                 
                               
                                
                                
                                The Nifty June 2023 futures closed at 18,740, a premium of 51.9 points compared with the Nifty's closing of 18,688.10 in the cash market.

In the cash market, the Nifty 50 lost 67.80 points or 0.36% to settle at 18,688.10.

The NSE's India VIX, a gauge of market's expectation of volatility over the near term, fell 0.73% to 11.08.
<style='margin-top:96px;'></style>
HDFC Bank, Reliance Industries (RIL) and ICICI Bank were the top traded individual stock futures contracts in F&O segment of NSE.

The June 2023 F&O contracts will expire on 29 June 2023.
