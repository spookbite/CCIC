                                       Nifty May futures trade at premium
                                    
                                    May 04, 2023 04:20 PM | Source: capitalmarket.com
                                        
                                    
                                        
                                         HDFC Bank, HDFC and RIL were most active in futures segment.
                                    
                                   
                                
                                 
                               
                                
                                
                                The Nifty May 2023 futures closed at 18,284, a premium of 28.2 points compared with the Nifty's closing of 18,255.80 in the cash market.

In the cash market, the Nifty 50 jumped 165.95 points or 0.92% to settle at 18,255.80.

The NSE's India VIX, a gauge of market's expectation of volatility over the near term, fell 0.90% to 11.73.
<style='margin-top:96px;'></style>
HDFC Bank, Housing Development Finance Corporation (HDFC) and Reliance Industries (RIL) were the top traded individual stock futures contracts in F&O segment of NSE.

The May 2023 F&O contracts will expire on 25 May 2023.
