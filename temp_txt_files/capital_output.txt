                                       Nifty August futures trade at premium
                                    
                                    Aug 04, 2023 04:43 PM | Source: capitalmarket.com
                                        
                                    
                                        
                                         SBI, RIL & ICICI Bank were the top traded contracts. 
                                    
                                   
                                
                                 
                               
                                
                                
                                
The Nifty August 2023 futures closed at 19,573.20, a premium of 56.2 points compared with the Nifty's closing 19,517 in the cash market.

In the cash market, the Nifty 50 advanced 135.35 points or 0.7% to settle at 19,517.
<style='margin-top:96px;'></style>
The NSE's India VIX, a gauge of market's expectation of volatility over the near term, tumbled 5.47% to 10.57.

State Bank of India, Reliance Industries and ICICI Bank were the top traded individual stock futures contracts in F&O segment of NSE.

The August 2023 F&O contracts will expire on 31 August 2023.
