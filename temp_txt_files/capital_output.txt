                                       Nifty April futures trade at premium
                                    
                                    Apr 21, 2023 04:14 PM | Source: capitalmarket.com
                                        
                                    
                                        
                                         ICICI Bank, RIL & HCL Tech were the top traded contracts. 
                                    
                                   
                                
                                 
                               
                                
                                
                                
The Nifty April 2023 futures closed at 17,648.10, a premium of 24.05 points compared with the Nifty's closing of 17,624.05 in the cash market.

In the cash market, the Nifty 50 lost 0.40 points to settle at 17,624.05.
<style='margin-top:96px;'></style>
The NSE's India VIX, a gauge of market's expectation of volatility over the near term, declined 2.59% to 11.63.

ICICI Bank, Reliance Industries and HCL Technologies were the top traded individual stock futures contracts in F&O segment of NSE.

The April 2023 F&O contracts will expire on 27 April 2023.
