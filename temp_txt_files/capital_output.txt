                                       Nifty Feb futures trade at premium
                                    
                                    Feb 16, 2023 04:23 PM | Source: capitalmarket.com
                                        
                                    
                                        
                                         RIL, Kotak Bank and TechM were the top traded contracts. 
                                    
                                   
                                
                                 
                               
                                
                                
                                
The Nifty February 2023 futures closed at 18,057.35, a premium of 21.5 points compared with the Nifty's closing of 18,035.85 in the cash market.

Turnover on the National Stock Exchange's futures & options (F&O) segment was Rs 343.05 lakh crore compared with Rs 170.08 lakh crore reported in the previous session.
<style='margin-top:96px;'></style>
In the cash market, the Nifty 50 gained 20 points or 0.11% to settle at 18,035.85.

The NSE's India VIX, a gauge of market's expectation of volatility over the near term, rose 0.23% to 12.89.

Reliance Industries (RIL), Kotak Mahindra Bank and Tech Mahindra were the top traded individual stock futures contracts in F&O segment of NSE.

The February 2023 F&O contracts will expire on 23 February 2023.
