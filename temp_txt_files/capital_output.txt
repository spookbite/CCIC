                                       Nifty April futures trade at premium
                                    
                                    Apr 10, 2023 04:23 PM | Source: capitalmarket.com
                                        
                                    
                                        
                                         NSE India VIX climbed 4.02% to 12.27.
                                    
                                   
                                
                                 
                               
                                
                                
                                
The Nifty April 2023 futures closed at 17,677, a premium of 52.95 points compared with the Nifty's closing of 17,624.05 in the cash market.

In the cash market, the Nifty 50 gained 24.90 points or 0.14% to settle at 17,624.05.
<style='margin-top:96px;'></style>
The NSE's India VIX, a gauge of market's expectation of volatility over the near term, jumped 4.02% to 12.27.

Tata Motors, ICICI Bank and Reliance Industries (RIL) were the top traded individual stock futures contracts in F&O segment of NSE.

The April 2023 F&O contracts will expire on 27 April 2023.
