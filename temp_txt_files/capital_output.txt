                                       Nifty July futures trade at premium
                                    
                                    Jul 17, 2023 04:36 PM | Source: capitalmarket.com
                                        
                                    
                                        
                                         NSE India VIX climbed 5.92% to 11.32.
                                    
                                   
                                
                                 
                               
                                
                                
                                
The Nifty July 2023 futures closed at 19,727, a premium of 15.55 points compared with the Nifty's closing of 19,711.45 in the cash market.
  
In the cash market, the Nifty 50 advanced 146.95 points or 0.75% to settle at 19,711.45.
<style='margin-top:96px;'></style>
The NSE's India VIX, a gauge of market's expectation of volatility over the near term, jumped 5.92% to 11.32.

HDFC Bank, Reliance Industries and Infosys were the top traded individual stock futures contracts in F&O segment of NSE.

The July 2023 F&O contracts will expire on 27 July 2023.
