                                       Nifty August futures trade at premium
                                    
                                    Aug 21, 2023 05:29 PM | Source: capitalmarket.com
                                        
                                    
                                        
                                         RIL, HDFC Bank & Adani Ports were the top traded contracts.
                                    
                                   
                                
                                 
                               
                                
                                
                                
The Nifty August 2023 futures closed at 19,399, a premium of 5.4 point compared with the Nifty's closing 19,393.60 in the cash market.

In the cash market, the Nifty 50 added 83.45 points or 0.43% to settle at 19,393.60.
<style='margin-top:96px;'></style>
The NSE's India VIX, a gauge of market's expectation of volatility over the near term, fell 1.50% to 11.96.

Reliance Industries (RIL), HDFC Bank and Adani Ports and Special Economic Zone were the top traded individual stock futures contracts in F&O segment of NSE.

The August 2023 F&O contracts will expire on 31 August 2023.
