                                       Nifty July futures trade at premium
                                    
                                    Jul 21, 2023 04:41 PM | Source: capitalmarket.com
                                        
                                    
                                        
                                         NSE India VIX slipped 2.54% to 11.49.
                                    
                                   
                                
                                 
                               
                                
                                
                                
The Nifty July 2023 futures closed at 19,806.20, a premium of 61.2points compared with the Nifty's closing 19,745 in the cash market.
  
In the cash market, the Nifty 50 dropped 234.15 points or 1.17% to settle at 19,745.
<style='margin-top:96px;'></style>
The NSE's India VIX, a gauge of market's expectation of volatility over the near term, declined 2.54% to 11.49.

Infosys, HDFC Bank, and Reliance Industries (RIL) were the top traded individual stock futures contracts in F&O segment of NSE.

The July 2023 F&O contracts will expire on 27 July 2023.
