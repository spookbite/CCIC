                                       Nifty August futures trade at premium
                                    
                                    Aug 09, 2023 04:34 PM | Source: capitalmarket.com
                                        
                                    
                                        
                                         NSE India VIX declined 1.63% as shares rallied.
                                    
                                   
                                
                                 
                               
                                
                                
                                
The Nifty August 2023 futures closed at 19,693, a premium of 60.45 points compared with the Nifty's closing 19,632.55 in the cash market.

In the cash market, the Nifty 50 gained 61.70 points or 0.32% to settle at 19,632.55.
<style='margin-top:96px;'></style>
The NSE's India VIX, a gauge of market's expectation of volatility over the near term, fell 1.63% to 11.14.

HDFC Bank, Reliance Industries and Bharat Forge were the top traded individual stock futures contracts in F&O segment of NSE.

The August 2023 F&O contracts will expire on 31 August 2023.
