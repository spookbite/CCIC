                                       Nifty April futures trade at premium
                                    
                                    Apr 12, 2023 04:21 PM | Source: capitalmarket.com
                                        
                                    
                                        
                                         NSE India VIX rose 2.48% to 12.27.
                                    
                                   
                                
                                 
                               
                                
                                
                                The Nifty April 2023 futures closed at 17,863.35, a premium of 50.95 points compared with the Nifty's closing of 17, 812.40 in the cash market.

In the cash market, the Nifty 50 gained 90.10 points or 0.51% to settle at 17,812.40.

The NSE's India VIX, a gauge of market's expectation of volatility over the near term, advanced 2.48% to 12.27.
<style='margin-top:96px;'></style>
Reliance Industries, Divi's Laboratories and Infosys were the top traded individual stock futures contracts in F&O segment of NSE.

The April 2023 F&O contracts will expire on 27 April 2023.
