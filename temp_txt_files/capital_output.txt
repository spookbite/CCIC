                                       Nifty September futures trade at premium
                                    
                                    Sep 12, 2023 03:59 PM | Source: capitalmarket.com
                                        
                                    
                                        
                                         HDFC Bank, RIL & ICICI Bank were the top traded contracts.
                                    
                                   
                                
                                 
                               
                                
                                
                                
The Nifty September 2023 futures closed at 20,035.15, a premium of 41.95 points compared with the Nifty's closing 19,993.20 in the cash market.

In the cash market, the Nifty 50 lost 3.15 points or 0.02% to settle at 19,993.20.
<style='margin-top:96px;'></style>
The NSE's India VIX, a gauge of market's expectation of volatility over the near term, advanced 3.01% to 11.69.

HDFC Bank, Reliance Industries (RIL) and ICICI Bank were the top traded individual stock futures contracts in F&O segment of NSE.

The September 2023 F&O contracts will expire on 28 September 2023.
