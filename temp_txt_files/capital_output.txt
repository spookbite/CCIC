                                       Nifty April futures trade at premium
                                    
                                    Apr 05, 2023 04:14 PM | Source: capitalmarket.com
                                        
                                    
                                        
                                         NSE India VIX declined 1.37% as shares rallied.
                                    
                                   
                                
                                 
                               
                                
                                
                                
The Nifty April 2023 futures closed at 17,625.35, a premium of 68.3 points compared with the Nifty's closing of 17,557.05 in the cash market.

In the cash market, the Nifty 50 jumped 159 points or 0.91% to settle at 17,557.05.
<style='margin-top:96px;'></style>
The NSE's India VIX, a gauge of market's expectation of volatility over the near term, fell 1.37% to 12.41.

HDFC Bank, Reliance Industries (RIL) and Bajaj Finance were the top traded individual stock futures contracts in F&O segment of NSE.

The April 2023 F&O contracts will expire on 27 April 2023.
