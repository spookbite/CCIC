                                       Nifty February futures trade at premium
                                    
                                    Jan 30, 2023 04:43 PM | Source: capitalmarket.com
                                        
                                    
                                        
                                         Adani Enterprises, Adani Ports and Special Economic Zone (APSEZ) and ICICI Bank were the top traded contracts.
                                    
                                   
                                
                                 
                               
                                
                                
                                
The Nifty February 2023 futures closed at a premium of 93.55 points compared with the Nifty's closing of 17,648.95 in the cash market.

Turnover on the National Stock Exchange's futures & options (F&O) segment was Rs 135.19 lakh crore compared with Rs 105.82 lakh crore reported in the previous session.
<style='margin-top:96px;'></style>
In the cash market, the Nifty 50 advanced 44.60 points or 0.25% to settle at 17,648.95

The NSE's India VIX, a gauge of market's expectation of volatility over the near term, rose 2.25% to 17.71.

Adani Enterprises, APSEZ and ICICI Bank were the top traded individual stock futures contracts in F&O segment of NSE.

The February 2023 F&O contracts will expire on 23 February 2023.
