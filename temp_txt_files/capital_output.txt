                                       Nifty July futures trade at premium
                                    
                                    Jul 10, 2023 05:21 PM | Source: capitalmarket.com
                                        
                                    
                                        
                                         RIL, HDFC Bank & TaMo were the traded contracts. 
                                    
                                   
                                
                                 
                               
                                
                                
                                
The Nifty July 2023 futures closed at 19,425, a premium of 69.1 points compared with the Nifty's closing of 19,355.90 in the cash market.

In the cash market, the Nifty 50 added 24.10 points or 0.12% to settle at 19,355.90.
<style='margin-top:96px;'></style>
The NSE's India VIX, a gauge of market's expectation of volatility over the near term, fell 0.60% to 11.46.

Reliance Industries, HDFC Bank and Tata Motors were the top traded individual stock futures contracts in F&O segment of NSE.

The July 2023 F&O contracts will expire on 27 July 2023.
