                                       Nifty May futures trade at premium 
                                    
                                    May 10, 2023 04:18 PM | Source: capitalmarket.com
                                        
                                    
                                        
                                         NSE India VIX jumped 3.19% to 13.08 
                                    
                                   
                                
                                 
                               
                                
                                
                                The Nifty May 2023 futures closed at 18,343, a premium of 27.9 points compared with the Nifty's closing of 18,315.10 in the cash market.

In the cash market, the Nifty 50 added 49.15 points or 0.27% to settle at 18,315.10.

The NSE's India VIX, a gauge of market's expectation of volatility over the near term, advanced 3.19% to 13.08.
<style='margin-top:96px;'></style>
HDFC Bank, ICICI Bank, State Bank of India were the top traded individual stock futures contracts in F&O segment of NSE.

The May 2023 F&O contracts will expire on 25 May 2023.
