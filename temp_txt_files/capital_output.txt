                                       Nifty March futures trade at premium
                                    
                                    Mar 01, 2023 04:14 PM | Source: capitalmarket.com
                                        
                                    
                                        
                                         NSE India VIX tumbled 7.31% as shares advanced.
                                    
                                   
                                
                                 
                               
                                
                                
                                The Nifty March 2023 futures closed at 17,538.95, a premium of 88.05 points compared with the Nifty's closing of 17,450.90 in the cash market.

Turnover on the National Stock Exchange's futures & options (F&O) segment was Rs 166.39 lakh crore compared with Rs 224.90 lakh crore reported in the previous session.

In the cash market, the Nifty 50 added 146.95 points or 0.85% to settle at 17,450.90.
<style='margin-top:96px;'></style>
The NSE's India VIX, a gauge of market's expectation of volatility over the near term, slumped 7.31% to 12.9950.

Adani Enterprises, Reliance Industries (RIL) and Adani Ports and Special Economic Zone were the top traded individual stock futures contracts in F&O segment of NSE.

The March 2023 F&O contracts will expire on 29 March 2023.
