                                       Nifty April futures trade at premium
                                    
                                    Apr 18, 2023 04:05 PM | Source: capitalmarket.com
                                        
                                    
                                        
                                         RIL, Infy and HDFC Bank were the top traded contracts. 
                                    
                                   
                                
                                 
                               
                                
                                
                                
The Nifty April 2023 futures closed at 17,709.10, a premium of 48.95 points compared with the Nifty's closing of 17,660.15 in the cash market.

In the cash market, the Nifty 50 lost 46.70 points or 0.26% to settle at 17,660.15.
<style='margin-top:96px;'></style>
The NSE's India VIX, a gauge of market's expectation of volatility over the near term, fell 1.54% to 46.70.

Reliance Industries (RIL), Infosys and HDFC Bank were the top traded individual stock futures contracts in F&O segment of NSE.

The April 2023 F&O contracts will expire on 27 April 2023.
