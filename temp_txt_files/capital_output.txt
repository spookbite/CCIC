                                       Nifty June futures trade at premium
                                    
                                    Jun 13, 2023 04:28 PM | Source: capitalmarket.com
                                        
                                    
                                        
                                         NSE India VIX declined 1.20% as shares advanced.
                                    
                                   
                                
                                 
                               
                                
                                
                                The Nifty June 2023 futures closed at 18,778, a premium of 61.85 points compared with the Nifty's closing of 18,716.15 in the cash market.

In the cash market, the Nifty 50 added 114.65 points or 0.62% to settle at 18,716.15.

The NSE's India VIX, a gauge of market's expectation of volatility over the near term, fell 1.20% to 11.11.
<style='margin-top:96px;'></style>
Reliance Industries (RIL), Zee Entertainment Enterprises (ZEEL) and ICICI Bank were the top traded individual stock futures contracts in F&O segment of NSE.

The June 2023 F&O contracts will expire on 29 June 2023.
