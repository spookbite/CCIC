                                       Nifty August futures trade at premium
                                    
                                    Aug 03, 2023 06:51 PM | Source: capitalmarket.com
                                        
                                    
                                        
                                         Vedanta, ICICI Bank & HDFC Bank were the top traded contracts.
                                    
                                   
                                
                                 
                               
                                
                                
                                
The Nifty August 2023 futures closed at 19,472, a premium of 90.35 points compared with the Nifty's closing 19,381.65 in the cash market.

In the cash market, the Nifty 50 declined 144.90 points or 0.74% to settle at 19,381.65.
<style='margin-top:96px;'></style>
The NSE's India VIX, a gauge of market's expectation of volatility over the near term, fell 0.82% to 11.19.

Vedanta, ICICI Bank and HDFC Bank were the top traded individual stock futures contracts in F&O segment of NSE.

The August 2023 F&O contracts will expire on 31 August 2023.
