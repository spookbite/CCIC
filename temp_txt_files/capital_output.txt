                                       Nifty February futures trade at premium
                                    
                                    Feb 09, 2023 04:41 PM | Source: capitalmarket.com
                                        
                                    
                                        
                                         NSE India VIX shed 4.08% as shares gained.
                                    
                                   
                                
                                 
                               
                                
                                
                                The Nifty February 2023 futures closed at 17,959.10, a premium of 36.95 points compared with the Nifty's closing of 17,893.45 in the cash market.

Turnover on the National Stock Exchange's futures & options (F&O) segment was Rs 337.55 lakh crore compared with Rs 170.03 lakh crore reported in the previous session.

In the cash market, the Nifty 50 added 21.75 points or 0.12% to settle at 17,893.45.
<style='margin-top:96px;'></style>
The NSE's India VIX, a gauge of market's expectation of volatility over the near term, declined 4.08% to 13.04.

Adani Enterprises, Adani Ports and Special Economic Zone and Reliance Industries were the top traded individual stock futures contracts in F&O segment of NSE.

The February 2023 F&O contracts will expire on 23 February 2023.
