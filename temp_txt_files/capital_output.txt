                                       Nifty January futures trade at premium
                                    
                                    Jan 11, 2023 04:36 PM | Source: capitalmarket.com
                                        
                                    
                                        
                                         Bharti Airtel, ICICI Bank and Reliance Industries (RIL) were the top traded contracts.
                                    
                                   
                                
                                 
                               
                                
                                
                                
The Nifty January 2023 futures closed at 17,960, a premium of 64.3 points compared with the Nifty's closing of 17,895.70 in the cash market.

Turnover on the National Stock Exchange's futures & options (F&O) segment was Rs 202.96 lakh crore compared with Rs 226.39 lakh crore reported in the previous session.
<style='margin-top:96px;'></style>
In the cash market, the Nifty 50 shed 18.45 points or 0.10% to settle at 17,895.70.

The NSE's India VIX, a gauge of market's expectation of volatility over the near term, declined 0.48% to 15.44.

Bharti Airtel, ICICI Bank and RIL were the top traded individual stock futures contracts in F&O segment of NSE.

The January 2023 F&O contracts will expire on 25 January 2023.
