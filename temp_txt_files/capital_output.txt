                                       Nifty August futures trade at discount
                                    
                                    Aug 22, 2023 05:19 PM | Source: capitalmarket.com
                                        
                                    
                                        
                                         HDFC Bank, RIL & ICICI Bank were the top traded contracts.
                                    
                                   
                                
                                 
                               
                                
                                
                                
The Nifty August 2023 futures closed at 19,381, a discount of 15.45 point compared with the Nifty's closing 19,396.45 in the cash market.

In the cash market, the Nifty 50 rose 2.85 points or 0.01% to settle at 19,396.45.
<style='margin-top:96px;'></style>
The NSE's India VIX, a gauge of market's expectation of volatility over the near term, declined 1.77% to 11.75.

HDFC Bank, Reliance Industries (RIL) and ICICI Bank were the top traded individual stock futures contracts in F&O segment of NSE.

The August 2023 F&O contracts will expire on 31 August 2023.
