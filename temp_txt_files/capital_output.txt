                                       Nifty August futures trade at discount
                                    
                                    Aug 23, 2023 04:41 PM | Source: capitalmarket.com
                                        
                                    
                                        
                                         HDFC Bank, RIL & ICICI Bank remained the top traded contracts for second day in a row.
                                    
                                   
                                
                                 
                               
                                
                                
                                
The Nifty August 2023 futures closed at 19,433, a discount of 11 points compared with the Nifty's closing 19,444 in the cash market.

In the cash market, the Nifty 50 added 47.55 points or 0.25% to settle at 19,444.
<style='margin-top:96px;'></style>
The NSE's India VIX, a gauge of market's expectation of volatility over the near term, fell 0.17% to 11.73.

HDFC Bank, Reliance Industries (RIL) and ICICI Bank were the top traded individual stock futures contracts in F&O segment of NSE.

The August 2023 F&O contracts will expire on 31 August 2023.
