                                       Nifty June futures trade at premium 
                                    
                                    May 29, 2023 04:01 PM | Source: capitalmarket.com
                                        
                                    
                                        
                                         RIL, HDFC Bank and ICICI Bank were the top traded contracts.
                                    
                                   
                                
                                 
                               
                                
                                
                                
The Nifty June 2023 futures closed at 18,673, a premium of 74.35 points compared with the Nifty's closing of 18,598.65 in the cash market.

In the cash market, the Nifty 50 added 99.30 points or 0.54% to settle at 18,598.65.
<style='margin-top:96px;'></style>
The NSE's India VIX, a gauge of market's expectation of volatility over the near term, rose 3.34% to 12.30.

Reliance Industries (RIL), HDFC Bank and ICICI Bank were the top traded individual stock futures contracts in F&O segment of NSE.

The June 2023 F&O contracts will expire on 29 June 2023.
