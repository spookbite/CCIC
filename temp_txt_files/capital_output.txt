                                       Nifty July futures trade at premium
                                    
                                    Jul 14, 2023 04:16 PM | Source: capitalmarket.com
                                        
                                    
                                        
                                         TCS, Infy & RIL were the traded contracts. 
                                    
                                   
                                
                                 
                               
                                
                                
                                
The Nifty July 2023 futures closed at 19,624, a premium of 59.5 points compared with the Nifty's closing of 19,564.50 in the cash market.

In the cash market, the Nifty 50 added 150.75 points or 0.78% to settle at 19,564.50.
<style='margin-top:96px;'></style>
The NSE's India VIX, a gauge of market's expectation of volatility over the near term, declined 2.33% to 10.68.

Tata Consultancy Services (TCS), Infosys (Infy) and Reliance Industries (RIL) were the top traded individual stock futures contracts in F&O segment of NSE.

The July 2023 F&O contracts will expire on 27 July 2023.
