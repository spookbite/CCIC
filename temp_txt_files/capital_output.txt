                                       Nifty July futures trade at premium
                                    
                                    Jul 18, 2023 04:51 PM | Source: capitalmarket.com
                                        
                                    
                                        
                                         NSE India VIX climbed 3.49% to 11.71.
                                    
                                   
                                
                                 
                               
                                
                                
                                
The Nifty July 2023 futures closed at 19,773, a premium of 23.75 points compared with the Nifty's closing of 19,749.25 in the cash market.
  
In the cash market, the Nifty 50 advanced 37.80 points or 0.19% to settle at 19,749.25.
<style='margin-top:96px;'></style>
The NSE's India VIX, a gauge of market's expectation of volatility over the near term, jumped 3.49% to 11.71.

HDFC Bank, Infosys and Reliance Industries (RIL) were the top traded individual stock futures contracts in F&O segment of NSE.

The July 2023 F&O contracts will expire on 27 July 2023.
