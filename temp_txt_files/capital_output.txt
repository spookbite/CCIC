                                       Nifty May futures trade at premium
                                    
                                    May 11, 2023 04:43 PM | Source: capitalmarket.com
                                        
                                    
                                        
                                         L&T, Asian Paints & Dr Reddy's Lab were the top traded contracts. 
                                    
                                   
                                
                                 
                               
                                
                                
                                
The Nifty May 2023 futures closed at 18,359, a premium of 62 points compared with the Nifty's closing of 18,297 in the cash market.

In the cash market, the Nifty 50 lost 18.10 points or 0.1% to settle at 18,297.
<style='margin-top:96px;'></style>
The NSE's India VIX, a gauge of market's expectation of volatility over the near term, rose 1.01% to 13.22.

Larsen & Toubro (L&T), Asian Paints and Dr. Reddy's Laboratories were the top traded individual stock futures contracts in F&O segment of NSE.

The May 2023 F&O contracts will expire on 25 May 2023.
