                                       Nifty August futures trade at premium
                                    
                                    Aug 24, 2023 06:02 PM | Source: capitalmarket.com
                                        
                                    
                                        
                                         RIL, HDFC Bank & Coforge were the top traded contracts. 
                                    
                                   
                                
                                 
                               
                                
                                
                                
The Nifty August 2023 futures closed at 19,390.50, a premium of 2.80 points compared with the Nifty's closing 19,386.70 in the cash market.

In the cash market, the Nifty 50 lost 57.30 points or 0.29% to settle at 19,386.70.
<style='margin-top:96px;'></style>
The NSE's India VIX, a gauge of market's expectation of volatility over the near term, fell 0.23% to 11.70.

Reliance Industries (RIL), HDFC Bank and Coforge were the top traded individual stock futures contracts in F&O segment of NSE.

The August 2023 F&O contracts will expire on 31 August 2023.
