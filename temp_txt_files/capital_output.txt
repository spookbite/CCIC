                                       Nifty April futures trade at premium
                                    
                                    Apr 26, 2023 04:32 PM | Source: capitalmarket.com
                                        
                                    
                                        
                                         Reliance Industries (RIL), ICICI Bank and HDFC Bank were the top traded contracts.
                                    
                                   
                                
                                 
                               
                                
                                
                                
The Nifty April 2023 futures closed at 17,823.50, a premium of 9.9points compared with the Nifty's closing of 17,813.60 in the cash market.

In the cash market, the Nifty 50 gained 44.35 points or 0.25% to settle at 17,813.60.
<style='margin-top:96px;'></style>
The NSE's India VIX, a gauge of market's expectation of volatility over the near term, rose 1.15% to 11.65.

RIL, ICICI Bank and HDFC Bank were the top traded individual stock futures contracts in F&O segment of NSE.

The April 2023 F&O contracts will expire on 27 April 2023.
