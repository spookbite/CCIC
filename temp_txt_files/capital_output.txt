                                       Nifty May futures trade at premium
                                    
                                    May 12, 2023 03:54 PM | Source: capitalmarket.com
                                        
                                    
                                        
                                         HDFC Bank, Eicher and TaMo were the top traded contracts. 
                                    
                                   
                                
                                 
                               
                                
                                
                                
The Nifty May 2023 futures closed at 18,332, a premium of 17.2 points compared with the Nifty's closing of 18,314.80 in the cash market.

In the cash market, the Nifty 50 added 17.80 points or 0.1% to settle at 18,314.80.
<style='margin-top:96px;'></style>
The NSE's India VIX, a gauge of market's expectation of volatility over the near term, fell 2.74% to 12.85.

HDFC Bank, Eicher Motors and Tata Motors were the top traded individual stock futures contracts in F&O segment of NSE.

The May 2023 F&O contracts will expire on 25 May 2023.
