                                       Nifty June futures trade at premium
                                    
                                    May 26, 2023 04:34 PM | Source: capitalmarket.com
                                        
                                    
                                        
                                         NSE India VIX tumbled 4.95% as shares rallied.
                                    
                                   
                                
                                 
                               
                                
                                
                                The Nifty June 2023 futures closed at 18,570.20, a premium of 70.85 points compared with the Nifty's closing of 18,499.35 in the cash market.

In the cash market, the Nifty 50 gained 178.20 points or 0.97% to settle at 18,499.35.

The NSE's India VIX, a gauge of market's expectation of volatility over the near term, slipped 4.95% to 11.90.
<style='margin-top:96px;'></style>
Reliance Industries, HDFC Bank and Page Industries were the top traded individual stock futures contracts in F&O segment of NSE.

The June 2023 F&O contracts will expire on 29 June 2023.
