                                       Nifty January futures trade at steep premium 
                                    
                                    Dec 29, 2022 04:29 PM | Source: capitalmarket.com
                                        
                                    
                                        
                                         RIL, Adani Ports and ICICI Bank were the top traded contracts. 
                                    
                                   
                                
                                 
                               
                                
                                
                                
The Nifty January 2023 futures closed at 18,299, a premium of 108 points compared with the Nifty's closing of 18,191 in the cash market.

Turnover on the National Stock Exchange's futures & options (F&O) segment was Rs 356.72 lakh crore compared with Rs 159.17 lakh crore reported in the previous session.
<style='margin-top:96px;'></style>
In the cash market, the Nifty 50 index added 68.50 points or 0.38% to settle at 18,191.

The NSE's India VIX, a gauge of market's expectation of volatility over the near term, fell 3.78% to 14.81.

Reliance Industries (RIL), ICICI Bank and Adani Ports and Special Economic Zone were the top traded individual stock futures contracts in F&O segment of NSE.

The January 2023 F&O contracts will expire on 25 January 2023.
