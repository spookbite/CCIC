                                       Nifty April futures trade at premium
                                    
                                    Apr 03, 2023 04:25 PM | Source: capitalmarket.com
                                        
                                    
                                        
                                         NSE India VIX declined 2.70% to 12.59.
                                    
                                   
                                
                                 
                               
                                
                                
                                
The Nifty April 2023 futures closed at 17,474.95, a premium of 76.9 points compared with the Nifty's closing of 17,398.05 in the cash market.

In the cash market, the Nifty 50 gained 38.30 points or 0.22% to settle at 17,398.05.
<style='margin-top:96px;'></style>
The NSE's India VIX, a gauge of market's expectation of volatility over the near term, slipped 2.70% to 12.59.

Reliance Industries (RIL), ICICI Bank and Adani Enterprises were the top traded individual stock futures contracts in F&O segment of NSE.

The April 2023 F&O contracts will expire on 27 April 2023.
