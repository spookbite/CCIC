                                       Nifty August futures trade at premium
                                    
                                    Aug 10, 2023 04:37 PM | Source: capitalmarket.com
                                        
                                    
                                        
                                         NSE India VIX rallied 2.33% as shares slipped.
                                    
                                   
                                
                                 
                               
                                
                                
                                
The Nifty August 2023 futures closed at 19,598.60, a premium of 55.5 points compared with the Nifty's closing 19,543.10 in the cash market.

In the cash market, the Nifty 50 lost 89.45 points or 0.46% to settle at 19,543.10.
<style='margin-top:96px;'></style>
The NSE's India VIX, a gauge of market's expectation of volatility over the near term, advanced 2.33% to 11.40.

ZEE Entertainment Enterprises, HDFC Bank and Reliance Industries were the top traded individual stock futures contracts in F&O segment of NSE.

The August 2023 F&O contracts will expire on 31 August 2023.
