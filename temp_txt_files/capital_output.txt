                                       Nifty July futures trade at premium
                                    
                                    Jul 25, 2023 04:17 PM | Source: capitalmarket.com
                                        
                                    
                                        
                                         NSE India VIX tumbled 12.10% to 10.24.
                                    
                                   
                                
                                 
                               
                                
                                
                                The Nifty July 2023 futures closed at 19,695, a premium of 14.40 points compared with the Nifty's closing 19,680.60 in the cash market.

In the cash market, the Nifty 50 added 8.25 points or 0.04% to settle at 19,680.60.

The NSE's India VIX, a gauge of market's expectation of volatility over the near term, slumped 12.10% to 10.24.
<style='margin-top:96px;'></style>
HDFC Bank, Axis Bank and ICICI Bank were the top traded individual stock futures contracts in F&O segment of NSE.

The July 2023 F&O contracts will expire on 27 July 2023.
