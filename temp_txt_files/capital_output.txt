                                       Nifty September futures trade at premium
                                    
                                    Sep 01, 2023 04:36 PM | Source: capitalmarket.com
                                        
                                    
                                        
                                         NSE India VIX dropped 5.78% as shares rallied.
                                    
                                   
                                
                                 
                               
                                
                                
                                
The Nifty September 2023 futures closed at 19,520.10, a premium of 84.8 points compared with the Nifty's closing 19,435.30 in the cash market.

In the cash market, the Nifty 50 advanced 181.50 points or 0.94% to settle at 19,435.30.
<style='margin-top:96px;'></style>
The NSE's India VIX, a gauge of market's expectation of volatility over the near term, slumped 5.78% to 11.37.

Reliance Industries, HDFC Bank and ICICI Bank were the top traded individual stock futures contracts in F&O segment of NSE.

The September 2023 F&O contracts will expire on 28 September 2023.
