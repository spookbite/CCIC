                                       Nifty April futures trade at premium
                                    
                                    Apr 13, 2023 04:17 PM | Source: capitalmarket.com
                                        
                                    
                                        
                                         NSE India VIX fell 2.97% as shares advanced.
                                    
                                   
                                
                                 
                               
                                
                                
                                The Nifty April 2023 futures closed at 17,884, a premium of 56 points compared with the Nifty's closing of 17,828 in the cash market.

In the cash market, the Nifty 50 gained 15.60 points or 0.09% to settle at 17,828.

The NSE's India VIX, a gauge of market's expectation of volatility over the near term, declined 2.97% to 11.91.
<style='margin-top:96px;'></style>
Infosys, Tata Consultancy Services (TCS) and AU Small Finance Bank were the top traded individual stock futures contracts in F&O segment of NSE.

The April 2023 F&O contracts will expire on 27 April 2023.
