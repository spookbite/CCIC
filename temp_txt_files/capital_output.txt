                                       Nifty July futures trade at premium
                                    
                                    Jul 06, 2023 04:17 PM | Source: capitalmarket.com
                                        
                                    
                                        
                                         RIL, HDFC Bank and Axis Bank were the top traded contracts.
                                    
                                   
                                
                                 
                               
                                
                                
                                The Nifty July 2023 futures closed at 19,532.95, a premium of 35.65 points compared with the Nifty's closing of 19,497.30 in the cash market.

In the cash market, the Nifty 50 advanced 98.90 points or 0.51% to settle at 19,497.30.

The NSE's India VIX, a gauge of market's expectation of volatility over the near term, fell 0.35% to 11.84.
<style='margin-top:96px;'></style>
Reliance Industries (RIL), HDFC Bank and Axis Bank were the top traded individual stock futures contracts in F&O segment of NSE.

The July 2023 F&O contracts will expire on 27 July 2023.
