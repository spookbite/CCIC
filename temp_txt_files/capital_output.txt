                                       Nifty May futures trade at premium
                                    
                                    May 19, 2023 04:18 PM | Source: capitalmarket.com
                                        
                                    
                                        
                                         NSE India VIX dropped 3.84% as shares rallied.
                                    
                                   
                                
                                 
                               
                                
                                
                                
The Nifty May 2023 futures closed at 18,230.50, a premium of 27.1points compared with the Nifty's closing of 18,203.40 in the cash market.

In the cash market, the Nifty 50 advanced 73.45 points or 0.41% to settle at 18,203.40.
<style='margin-top:96px;'></style>
The NSE's India VIX, a gauge of market's expectation of volatility over the near term, slipped 3.84% to 12.30.

State Bank of India (SBI), HDFC Bank and Adani Enterprises were the top traded individual stock futures contracts in F&O segment of NSE.

The May 2023 F&O contracts will expire on 25 May 2023.
