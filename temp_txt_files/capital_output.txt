                                       Nifty May futures trade at premium
                                    
                                    May 17, 2023 04:13 PM | Source: capitalmarket.com
                                        
                                    
                                        
                                         NSE India VIX fell 1.41% to 13.11.
                                    
                                   
                                
                                 
                               
                                
                                
                                
The Nifty May 2023 futures closed at 18,232, a premium of 50.25 points compared with the Nifty's closing of 18,181.75 in the cash market.

In the cash market, the Nifty 50 slipped 104.75 points or 0.57% to settle at 18,181.75.
<style='margin-top:96px;'></style>
The NSE's India VIX, a gauge of market's expectation of volatility over the near term, declined 1.41% to 13.11.

ICICI Bank, HDFC Bank and Jubilant FoodWorks were the top traded individual stock futures contracts in F&O segment of NSE.

The May 2023 F&O contracts will expire on 25 May 2023.
