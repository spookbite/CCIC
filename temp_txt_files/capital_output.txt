                                       Nifty April futures trade at premium
                                    
                                    Apr 06, 2023 04:18 PM | Source: capitalmarket.com
                                        
                                    
                                        
                                         NSE India VIX dropped 4.95% as shares advanced.
                                    
                                   
                                
                                 
                               
                                
                                
                                
The Nifty April 2023 futures closed at 17,650.60, a premium of 51.45 points compared with the Nifty's closing of 17,599.15 in the cash market.

In the cash market, the Nifty 50 gained 42.10 points or 0.24% to settle at 17,599.15.
<style='margin-top:96px;'></style>
The NSE's India VIX, a gauge of market's expectation of volatility over the near term, tumbled 4.95% to 11.80.

Reliance Industries (RIL), ICICI Bank and HDFC Bank were the top traded individual stock futures contracts in F&O segment of NSE.

The April 2023 F&O contracts will expire on 27 April 2023.
