                                       Nifty September futures trade at premium
                                    
                                    Sep 07, 2023 04:54 PM | Source: capitalmarket.com
                                        
                                    
                                        
                                         HDFC Bank, RIL & Coal India were the top traded contracts.
                                    
                                   
                                
                                 
                               
                                
                                
                                The Nifty September 2023 futures closed at 19,767.05, a premium of 40 points compared with the Nifty's closing 19,727.05 in the cash market.

In the cash market, the Nifty 50 rose 116 points or 0.59% to settle at 19,727.05.

The NSE's India VIX, a gauge of market's expectation of volatility over the near term, rose 1.82% to 10.87.
<style='margin-top:96px;'></style>
HDFC Bank, Reliance Industries (RIL) and Coal India were the top traded individual stock futures contracts in F&O segment of NSE.

The September 2023 F&O contracts will expire on 28 September 2023.
