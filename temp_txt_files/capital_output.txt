                                       Nifty August futures trade at premium
                                    
                                    Jul 31, 2023 04:42 PM | Source: capitalmarket.com
                                        
                                    
                                        
                                         HDFC Bank, PEL and Infy were the top traded contracts.
                                    
                                   
                                
                                 
                               
                                
                                
                                
The Nifty August 2023 futures closed at 19,837, a premium of 83.2 points compared with the Nifty's closing 19,753.80 in the cash market.

In the cash market, the Nifty 50 added 107.75 points or 0.55% to settle at 19,753.80.
<style='margin-top:96px;'></style>
The NSE's India VIX, a gauge of market's expectation of volatility over the near term, rose 2.73% to 10.41.

HDFC Bank, Piramal Enterprises and Infosys were the top traded individual stock futures contracts in F&O segment of NSE.

The August 2023 F&O contracts will expire on 31 August 2023.
