                                       Nifty March futures trade at premium
                                    
                                    Feb 23, 2023 04:18 PM | Source: capitalmarket.com
                                        
                                    
                                        
                                         NSE India VIX slipped 3.28% to 15.08.
                                    
                                   
                                
                                 
                               
                                
                                
                                
The Nifty March 2023 futures closed at 17,628.90, a premium of 117.65 points compared with the Nifty's closing of 17,511.25 in the cash market.

Turnover on the National Stock Exchange's futures & options (F&O) segment was Rs 403.97 lakh crore compared with Rs 190.08 lakh crore reported in the previous session.
<style='margin-top:96px;'></style>
In the cash market, the Nifty 50 shed 43.05 points or 0.25% to settle at 17,511.25.

The NSE's India VIX, a gauge of market's expectation of volatility over the near term, dropped 3.28% to 15.08.

Reliance Industries (RIL), ICICI Bank and Adani Enterprises were the top traded individual stock futures contracts in F&O segment of NSE.

The March 2023 F&O contracts will expire on 29 March 2023.
