                                       Nifty September futures trade at premium
                                    
                                    Sep 11, 2023 04:23 PM | Source: capitalmarket.com
                                        
                                    
                                        
                                         RIL, Adani Ports and HDFC Bank were the top traded contracts. 
                                    
                                   
                                
                                 
                               
                                
                                
                                
The Nifty September 2023 futures closed at 20,046, a premium of 49.65 points compared with the Nifty's closing 19,996.35 in the cash market.

In the cash market, the Nifty 50 added 176.40 points or 0.89% to settle at 19,996.35.
<style='margin-top:96px;'></style>
The NSE's India VIX, a gauge of market's expectation of volatility over the near term, jumped 5.26% to 11.35.

Reliance Industries (RIL), Adani Ports and Special Economic Zone, and HDFC Bank were the top traded individual stock futures contracts in F&O segment of NSE.

The September 2023 F&O contracts will expire on 28 September 2023.
