                                       Nifty March futures trade at premium
                                    
                                    Mar 28, 2023 04:20 PM | Source: capitalmarket.com
                                        
                                    
                                        
                                         HDFC Bank, RIL and ICICI Bank were the top traded contracts.  
                                    
                                   
                                
                                 
                               
                                
                                
                                
The Nifty March 2023 futures closed at 16,977.70, a premium of 26 points compared with the Nifty's closing of 16,951.70 in the cash market.

Turnover on the National Stock Exchange's futures & options (F&O) segment was Rs 245.96 lakh crore compared with Rs 181.72 lakh crore reported in the previous session.
<style='margin-top:96px;'></style>
In the cash market, the Nifty 50 declined 34 points or 0.20% to settle at 16,951.70.

The NSE's India VIX, a gauge of market's expectation of volatility over the near term, fell 2.23% to 15.1000.

HDFC Bank, Reliance Industries (RIL) and ICICI Bank were the top traded individual stock futures contracts in F&O segment of NSE.

The March 2023 F&O contracts will expire on 29 March 2023.
