                                       Nifty June futures trade at steep premium
                                    
                                    May 31, 2023 03:53 PM | Source: capitalmarket.com
                                        
                                    
                                        
                                         Kotak Bank, RIL & HDFC Bank were the top traded contracts.
                                    
                                   
                                
                                 
                               
                                
                                
                                
The Nifty June 2023 futures closed at 18,624.95, a premium of 90.55 points compared with the Nifty's closing of 18,534.40 in the cash market.

In the cash market, the Nifty 50 lost 99.45 points or 0.53% to settle at 18,534.40.
<style='margin-top:96px;'></style>
The NSE's India VIX, a gauge of market's expectation of volatility over the near term, declined 0.31% to 11.97.

Kotak Mahindra Bank, Reliance Industries and HDFC Bank were the top traded individual stock futures contracts in F&O segment of NSE.

The June 2023 F&O contracts will expire on 29 June 2023.
