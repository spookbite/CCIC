                                       Nifty January futures trade at premium
                                    
                                    Jan 03, 2023 04:13 PM | Source: capitalmarket.com
                                        
                                    
                                        
                                         Reliance Industries (RIL), Axis Bank and ICICI Bank were the top traded contracts.
                                    
                                   
                                
                                 
                               
                                
                                
                                The Nifty January 2023 futures closed at 18,315, a premium of 82.45 points compared with the Nifty's closing of 18,232.55 in the cash market.

Turnover on the National Stock Exchange's futures & options (F&O) segment was Rs 188.42 lakh crore compared with Rs 116.06 lakh crore reported in the previous session.

In the cash market, the Nifty 50 advanced 35.10 points or 0.19% to settle at 18,232.55.
<style='margin-top:96px;'></style>
The NSE's India VIX, a gauge of market's expectation of volatility over the near term, declined 2.04% to 14.3850.

RIL, Axis Bank and ICICI Bank were the top traded individual stock futures contracts in F&O segment of NSE.

The January 2022 F&O contracts will expire on 25 January 2023.
