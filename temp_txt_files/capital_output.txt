                                       Nifty March futures trade at premium
                                    
                                    Mar 14, 2023 04:29 PM | Source: capitalmarket.com
                                        
                                    
                                        
                                         Adani Enterprises, Reliance Industries (RIL) and ICICI Bank were the top traded contracts.
                                    
                                   
                                
                                 
                               
                                
                                
                                
The Nifty March 2023 futures closed at 17,140, a premium of 96.7 points compared with the Nifty's closing of 17,043.30 in the cash market.

Turnover on the National Stock Exchange's futures & options (F&O) segment was Rs 290.47 lakh crore compared with Rs 212.26 lakh crore reported in the previous session.
<style='margin-top:96px;'></style>
In the cash market, the Nifty 50 lost 111 points or 0.65% to settle at 17,043.30.

The NSE's India VIX, a gauge of market's expectation of volatility over the near term, added 0.01% to 16.22.

Adani Enterprises, RIL and ICICI Bank were the top traded individual stock futures contracts in F&O segment of NSE.

The March 2023 F&O contracts will expire on 29 March 2023.
