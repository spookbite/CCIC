                                       Nifty June futures trade at premium
                                    
                                    Jun 14, 2023 04:18 PM | Source: capitalmarket.com
                                        
                                    
                                        
                                         RIL, HDFC Bank and ICICI Bank were the top traded contracts.
                                    
                                   
                                
                                 
                               
                                
                                
                                The Nifty June 2023 futures closed at 18,811, a premium of 55.1 points compared with the Nifty's closing of 18,755.90 in the cash market.

In the cash market, the Nifty 50 added 39.75 points or 0.21% to settle at 18,755.90.

The NSE's India VIX, a gauge of market's expectation of volatility over the near term, rose 0.47% to 11.16.
<style='margin-top:96px;'></style>
Reliance Industries (RIL), HDFC Bank and ICICI Bank were the top traded individual stock futures contracts in F&O segment of NSE.

The June 2023 F&O contracts will expire on 29 June 2023.
