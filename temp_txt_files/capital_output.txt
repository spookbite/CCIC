                                       Nifty January futures trade at premium
                                    
                                    Jan 20, 2023 04:28 PM | Source: capitalmarket.com

                                The Nifty January 2023 futures closed at 18,055.95, a premium of 28.30 points compared with the Nifty's closing of 18,027.65 in the cash market.

Turnover on the National Stock Exchange's futures & options (F&O) segment was Rs 96.49 lakh crore compared with Rs 324.44 lakh crore reported in the previous session.

In the cash market, the Nifty 50 lost 80.20 points or 0.44% to settle at 18,027.65.
