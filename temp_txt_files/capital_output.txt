                                       Nifty March futures trade at premium
                                    
                                    Mar 21, 2023 04:16 PM | Source: capitalmarket.com
                                        
                                    
                                        
                                         NSE India VIX slides 5.77% as shares advanced.
                                    
                                   
                                
                                 
                               
                                
                                
                                The Nifty March 2023 futures closed at 17,165, a premium of 57.5 points compared with the Nifty's closing of 17,107.50 in the cash market.

Turnover on the National Stock Exchange's futures & options (F&O) segment was Rs 247.18 lakh crore compared with Rs 173.77 lakh crore reported in the previous session.

In the cash market, the Nifty 50 gained 119.10 points or 0.70% to settle at 17,107.50.
<style='margin-top:96px;'></style>
The NSE's India VIX, a gauge of market's expectation of volatility over the near term, slipped 5.77% to 15.0825.

Reliance Industries (RIL), ICICI Bank and Kotak Mahindra Bank were the top traded individual stock futures contracts in F&O segment of NSE.

The March 2023 F&O contracts will expire on 29 March 2023.
