                                       Nifty August Futures Trade At discount
                                    
                                    Aug 30, 2023 04:29 PM | Source: capitalmarket.com
                                        
                                    
                                        
                                         NSE India VIX tumbled 3.47% to 11.80.
                                    
                                   
                                
                                 
                               
                                
                                
                                The Nifty August 2023 futures closed at 19,340, a discount of 7.45 points compared with the Nifty's closing 19,347.45 in the cash market.

In the cash market, the Nifty 50 added 4.80 points or 0.02% to settle at 19,347.45.

The NSE's India VIX, a gauge of market's expectation of volatility over the near term, tumbled 3.47% to 11.80.

Reliance Industries, ICICI Bank and HDFC Bank were the top traded individual stock futures contracts in F&O segment of NSE.

The August 2023 F&O contracts will expire on 31 August 2023.
