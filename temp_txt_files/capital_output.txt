                                       Nifty September futures trade at premium
                                    
                                    Sep 04, 2023 04:38 PM | Source: capitalmarket.com
                                        
                                    
                                        
                                         NSE India VIX declined 3.54% as shares rallied.
                                    
                                   
                                
                                 
                               
                                
                                
                                The Nifty September 2023 futures closed at 19,613, a premium of 84.2 points compared with the Nifty's closing 19,528.80 in the cash market.

In the cash market, the Nifty 50 advanced 93.50 points or 0.48% to settle at 19,528.80.

The NSE's India VIX, a gauge of market's expectation of volatility over the near term, slipped 3.54% to 10.96.
<style='margin-top:96px;'></style>
Reliance Industries, HDFC Bank and Kotak Mahindra Bank were the top traded individual stock futures contracts in F&O segment of NSE.

The September 2023 F&O contracts will expire on 28 September 2023.
