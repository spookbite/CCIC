                                       Nifty May futures trade at premium
                                    
                                    May 05, 2023 04:10 PM | Source: capitalmarket.com
                                        
                                    
                                        
                                         NSE India VIX surged 4.79% as shares tumbled.
                                    
                                   
                                
                                 
                               
                                
                                
                                The Nifty May 2023 futures closed at 18,130.10, a premium of 61.1 points compared with the Nifty's closing of 18,069 in the cash market.

In the cash market, the Nifty 50 lost 186.80 points or 1.02% to settle at 18,069.

The NSE's India VIX, a gauge of market's expectation of volatility over the near term, jumped 4.79% to 12.30.
<style='margin-top:96px;'></style>
HDFC Bank, ICICI Bank and IndusInd Bank were the top traded individual stock futures contracts in F&O segment of NSE.

The May 2023 F&O contracts will expire on 25 May 2023.
