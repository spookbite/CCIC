                                       Nifty February futures trade at premium 
                                    
                                    Feb 01, 2023 05:48 PM | Source: capitalmarket.com
                                        
                                    
                                        
                                         Adani Ports, ICICI Bank, and AdaniEnt were the top traded contracts.
                                    
                                   
                                
                                 
                               
                                
                                
                                
The Nifty February 2023 futures closed at 17,665, a premium of 48.7 points compared with the Nifty's closing of 17,616.30 in the cash market.

Turnover on the National Stock Exchange's futures & options (F&O) segment was Rs 282.61 lakh crore compared with Rs 178.55 lakh crore reported in the previous session.
<style='margin-top:96px;'></style>
In the cash market, the Nifty 50 shed 45.85 points or 0.26% to settle at 17,616.30.

The NSE's India VIX, a gauge of market's expectation of volatility over the near term, declined 0.56% to 16.78.

Adani Ports and Special Economic Zone, ICICI Bank, Adani Enterprises were the top traded individual stock futures contracts in F&O segment of NSE.

The February 2023 F&O contracts will expire on 23 February 2023.
