                                       Nifty June futures trade at premium 
                                    
                                    Jun 01, 2023 04:36 PM | Source: capitalmarket.com
                                        
                                    
                                        
                                         NSE India VIX declined 3.13% to 11.06 
                                    
                                   
                                
                                 
                               
                                
                                
                                The Nifty June 2023 futures closed at 18,574.80, a premium of 87.05 points compared with the Nifty's closing of 18,487.75 in the cash market.

In the cash market, the Nifty 50 declined 46.65 points or 0.25% to settle at 18,487.75.

The NSE's India VIX, a gauge of market's expectation of volatility over the near term, dropped 3.13% to 11.06.

Kotak Mahindra Bank, HDFC Bank, Reliance Industries were the top traded individual stock futures contracts in F&O segment of NSE.
