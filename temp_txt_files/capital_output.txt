                                       Nifty April futures trade at premium
                                    
                                    Apr 24, 2023 04:38 PM | Source: capitalmarket.com
                                        
                                    
                                        
                                         ICICI Bank, Reliance Industries (RIL) and HDFC Bank were the top traded contracts.
                                    
                                   
                                
                                 
                               
                                
                                
                                
The Nifty April 2023 futures closed at 17,767.55, a premium of 24.15 points compared with the Nifty's closing of 17,743.40 in the cash market.

In the cash market, the Nifty 50 gained 119.35 points or 0.68% to settle at 17,743.40.
<style='margin-top:96px;'></style>
The NSE's India VIX, a gauge of market's expectation of volatility over the near term, rose 0.32% to 11.67.

ICICI Bank, RIL and HDFC Bank were the top traded individual stock futures contracts in F&O segment of NSE.

The April 2023 F&O contracts will expire on 27 April 2023.
