                                       Nifty January futures trade at premium
                                    
                                    Jan 06, 2023 04:35 PM | Source: capitalmarket.com
                                        
                                    
                                        
                                         ICICI Bank, Bajaj Finance and Reliance Industries (RIL) were the top traded contracts.
                                    
                                   
                                
                                 
                               
                                
                                
                                The Nifty January 2023 futures closed at 17,949.05, a premium of 89.6 points compared with the Nifty's closing of 17,859.45 in the cash market.

Turnover on the National Stock Exchange's futures & options (F&O) segment was Rs 128.11 lakh crore compared with Rs 418.61 lakh crore reported in the previous session.

In the cash market, the Nifty 50 declined 132.70 points or 0.74% to settle at 17,859.45.
<style='margin-top:96px;'></style>
The NSE's India VIX, a gauge of market's expectation of volatility over the near term, rose 0.28% to 15.025.

ICICI Bank, Bajaj Finance and RIL were the top traded individual stock futures contracts in F&O segment of NSE.

The January 2023 F&O contracts will expire on 25 January 2023.
