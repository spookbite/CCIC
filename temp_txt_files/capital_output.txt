                                       Nifty April futures trade at premium
                                    
                                    Apr 20, 2023 04:08 PM | Source: capitalmarket.com
                                        
                                    
                                        
                                         HDFC Bank, Infy and ICICI Bank were the top traded contracts. 
                                    
                                   
                                
                                 
                               
                                
                                
                                
The Nifty April 2023 futures closed at 17,655, a premium of 30.55 points compared with the Nifty's closing of 17,624.45 in the cash market.

In the cash market, the Nifty 50 added 5.70 points or 0.03% to settle at 17,624.45.
<style='margin-top:96px;'></style>
The NSE's India VIX, a gauge of market's expectation of volatility over the near term, fell 1.74% to 11.94.

HDFC Bank, Infosys and ICICI Bank were the top traded individual stock futures contracts in F&O segment of NSE.

The April 2023 F&O contracts will expire on 27 April 2023.
