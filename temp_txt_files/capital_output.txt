                                       Nifty May futures trade at premium
                                    
                                    May 24, 2023 04:13 PM | Source: capitalmarket.com
                                        
                                    
                                        
                                         NSE India VIX jumped 4.02% as shares slipped.
                                    
                                   
                                
                                 
                               
                                
                                
                                The Nifty May 2023 futures closed at 18,300.45, a premium of 15.05 points compared with the Nifty's closing of 18,285.40 in the cash market.

In the cash market, the Nifty 50 lost 62.60 points or 0.34% to settle at 18,285.40.

The NSE's India VIX, a gauge of market's expectation of volatility over the near term, surged 4.02% to 13.11.
<style='margin-top:96px;'></style>
Adani Enterprises, Reliance Industries and ICICI Bank were the top traded individual stock futures contracts in F&O segment of NSE.

The May 2023 F&O contracts will expire on 25 May 2023.
